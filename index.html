The Solution

Public Opinions media search is useful to investors when researching a company. Simply type in a company and Public Opinions searches all relevant social media and news platforms and gathers all the information into one place. Public Opinions also determines the markets sentiment, and counts the number of posts, articles, and videos for each stock so traders can know which stocks have the most positive and negative opinions and news at any given time.
